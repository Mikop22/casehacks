The Problem Customer & Business Context

The Gap

Scotiabank faces a structural gap converting younger Canadians (18–34) into active investors — despite the largest intergenerational wealth transfer in Canadian history.

  • $1 Trillion+ in wealth expected to transfer to younger Canadians
  • Many lack confidence and direction to begin investing
  • 82% of investors aged 18–24 use social media for investment information
  • This demographic relies on creators and influencers, not financial advisors
Young Canadians are interested in investing — but do not trust traditional banking entry points.

App Feature — Momentum Nudges Product Workflow

Behavior-Based Nudges

Instead of encouraging risky speculation, Launchpad uses behavior-anchored prompts that reinforce consistency:

Streak
"You've invested 3 weeks in a row — keep your streak?"
Goal Progress
"You're $20 away from your monthly goal."
Payday Trigger
"Your paycheck just hit — add $10?"
Mirrors subscription product engagement — avoids speculation, rewards consistency.
